Integrated approach in science and technology for a sustainable future

Sustainability is development of services, goods, and products that involves meeting our present needs without compromising the ability of future generations to fulfil their needs. It improves the quality of our lives, preserves natural resources for future generations and protects our ecosystem. Innovations in the field of clean transportation, smart cities and renewable energy are allowing better resource management and reducing dependence on fossil fuels and other non-renewable energy. In the coming years, companies across industries will have to continue to prioritize environmental issues and design, test and manufacture technologies that would help them. In this webinar we will discuss about integrated approach in science and technology for a sustainable future.

Event Speaker: Arvind Dhawan

26th February 2022 | 7 PM IST
